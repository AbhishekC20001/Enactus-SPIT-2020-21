About Us

Enactus is an international non-profit organization of students present in over 1730 campuses, spread across 39 countries and has over 72,000 students as active members who make an impact in the community, not in the form of charity, but by using the positive power of entrepreneurship. We are Enactus S.P.I.T., the student chapter of Enactus India at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan's Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, Andheri (W), Mumbai. We started in August 2020 and have been up and running energetically ever since. We work for the principle of social entrepreneurship to strengthen the people around us and the planet we live in; with regard to employability, environmental issues and development. We live and work to create a positive, long lasting impact.
