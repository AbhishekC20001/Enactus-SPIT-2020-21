Project Prithvi

Project Prithvi (means Earth) aims to reduce the use of plastic and replace it with alternate, eco-friendly materials. It also aims to popularize the use of natural or eco-friendly products. Through our project, we sell products made of bamboo, to reduce single-use plastic in everyday life by collaborating with Bamboo India (an agricultural start-up in India initiated by Ms. Ashwini Shinde and Mr. Yogesh Shinde). We also sell hand-made cotton masks (in collaboration with StreeStyle) to curb the increase of plastic waste from single use plastic masks as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
